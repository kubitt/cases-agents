## Format rotation (don't ship five of the same)
- useful — a tip, how-to, or checklist
- engaging — a question, poll, or hot take
- selling — a soft pitch tied to a real benefit + link
- entertaining — a light story, meme idea, or relatable moment

## Hook formulas (first line)
- [FILL DURING ONBOARDING or use defaults]
- "Your [thing] tastes/looks flat? It's almost always [cause]..."
- "[Number] [things] people get wrong about [topic]"
- "Stop doing [common mistake]. Do [better] instead."
- "Nobody talks about [overlooked thing], but..."

## Platform rules (hard limits)
- Telegram: no hashtags, longer ok, 1 link
- VK: ≤3 hashtags
- Instagram: ≤10 hashtags, line breaks
- X: ≤280 characters
- LinkedIn: professional, one insight/lesson
