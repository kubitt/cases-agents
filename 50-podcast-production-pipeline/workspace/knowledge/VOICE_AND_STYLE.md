## Worked example
- Overall voice: conversational, warm, dry humor; talks to one listener, not a crowd
- Sentence length: short, 6–14 words; Reading level: plain
- Words we like: "actually", "the honest version", "here's the catch"
- Banned: "without further ado", "buckle up", "game-changer", all-caps titles
- Emoji: promo only, max 1–2; Keyword themes: fundraising, pivots, first hires
- Hashtags: #startups #founders #podcast + one topic tag

## Rules
