## Reality Signal bands (higher = more crowded)
- 0–30: open space → verdict build (few credible competitors; confirm demand exists at all)
- 31–60: moderate → verdict differentiate (real space, needs a clear wedge)
- 61–80: crowded → verdict niche (many players; win a narrow slice or not at all)
- 81–100: saturated → verdict reconsider (giants entrenched; test a wedge or consider build-vs-contribute)

## Trend read
- accelerating: many recent launches or rising discussion | stable: steady, mature | declining: little recent activity, dead projects.
- Always an estimate from public evidence; label it so, and never call an idea guaranteed or hopeless.

## Concept-brief structure
- Sections in order: Problem & target user; Value proposition; Competitor landscape (each with a source link); Differentiation angle; Top risks & assumptions (3–5, each with a cheap test); Next steps (prioritized, smallest proving step first).
