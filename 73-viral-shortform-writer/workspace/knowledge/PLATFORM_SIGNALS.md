## What ER means (defined once, used everywhere)
- ER (engagement rate) = interactions divided by views (or by followers when views are hidden).

- Reels (Instagram) — sends + saves: make it worth sharing to a friend and saving for later.
- Shorts (YouTube) — retention / viewed-vs-swiped: earn the swipe-in and hold attention to the end.
- Threads — replies + reshares: write something people answer and repost.
- Xiaohongshu (小红书) — saves (收藏 / shōucáng) + comments: make it a keeper worth saving and discussing.
