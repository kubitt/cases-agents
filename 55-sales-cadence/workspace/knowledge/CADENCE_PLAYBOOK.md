## Cadence (default, editable)
- Touch timing: day 0 (T1 opener), day 3 (T2 new angle), day 7 (T3 usefulness, no ask), day 12 (T4 optional break-up).
- Max touches without a reply: 3, then next_action=wait and a manual call decision.
- Style: teach-and-reframe — each touch gives the buyer a reason to reply, never "just checking in".
- Length: 60–80 words, one CTA question.

## Stage definitions (worked example)
- new: added, not yet contacted.
- contacted: first touch sent, no reply yet.
- engaged: replied at least once (any genuine reply resets touch_count to 0).
- proposal: pricing or proposal sent.
- won / lost: closed. do_not_contact: opted out — permanent, never re-add.

Example: Acme Corp — engaged — asked about pricing on day 5 — next_touch set for +3 days, next_action=email (send pricing + propose a call).

## Never
- No fabricated personalization, no fake urgency, no bought lists.
- Never re-contact a do_not_contact lead, even if asked later.
