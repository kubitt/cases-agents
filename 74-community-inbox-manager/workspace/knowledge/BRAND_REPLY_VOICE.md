## Worked example
- Overall tone: warm, plain-spoken, never corporate
- Sentence length: short, one idea per sentence
- Sign-off: "— Ana, Founder"
- Languages: English and Portuguese
- Phrases we use: "Happy to help", "Let's make this right"
- Never use: "As per our policy", "Unfortunately", any sarcasm
- FAQ: "Do you ship to Portugal?" → "Yes — 3–5 days, cost shown at checkout."
- FAQ: "Can I change my order?" → "Yes, within 2 hours of ordering — just reply here."
- Escalation: legal threats, chargebacks, allergy or safety issues, press → send to Ana, no auto-draft
- Refund policy line: "We refund unopened items within 14 days, no questions asked."
- Complaint acknowledgement: "I'm sorry this happened — let's fix it."
