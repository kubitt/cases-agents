## RAG thresholds
(applied by skill kpi-tracking; a metric's direction lives in BUSINESS_PROFILE.md)
- green: metric meets or beats target
- amber: within the band below target — default band [FILL DURING ONBOARDING, default 10%]
- red: beyond the amber band
- lower-is-better metrics (churn, CAC): mirror the logic — green at/under target, red well over
- insufficient data: too little to read a status → flag it, do not color it

## Report sections
- Weekly brief: overall RAG headline, up to 3 decisions needed, KPI status list, plan progress, week ahead
- Meeting brief: who is in the room, the goal, 2–3 relevant numbers, the decision likely needed
- Plan one-pager: context, goal + primary metric, initiatives, risks, timeline

## Example filled
- Primary reader: founder + 3-person board | Formality: plain, board-ready, no hype | Length: one screen briefs, one page plans
- RAG band: amber = within 10% of target; red = worse than 10% off
- Note learned: the board wants the "decisions needed" list first and dollar figures rounded to the nearest \$100.
