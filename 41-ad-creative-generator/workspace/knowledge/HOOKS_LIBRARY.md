## The five hooks
- pain — open with the problem the reader feels ("Losing an hour a day to X?")
- benefit — open with the result they get ("Get X back in Y days")
- social proof — a real number or case ("500 teams cut X with...") — only if true
- urgency — a real deadline or limit ("Ends April 30") — only if real
- curiosity — an open loop that begs the click ("The X fix most people miss")

## Format rules (defaults; override per platform in the brief)
- headline ≤ 30 characters
- primary text ≤ 90 characters
- description ≤ 90 characters
- one clear CTA per ad

## Honesty rules
- Real numbers only; no invented stats or fake reviews
- Banned words: best, unique, top, guaranteed, revolutionary (+ brand additions)
- Urgency only with a real deadline; social proof only with a real number
